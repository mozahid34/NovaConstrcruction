Loft Conversion Staircase Building Regulations:

There are strict building regulations that must be followed while building anything into your home. Building regulations is one of the reasons why conducting proper research is essential before you start building. As a loft conversion drastically changes the structure of a house, the building regulations must be followed strictly. The loft conversion staircase is one of the key parts of the building regulations. You must take the headroom, quality, and pitch into consideration while building the staircase.

  • The stairs in the house must be fixed
  • The maximum steep pitch is 42 degrees
  • The staircase risers have to be of equal level
